Features

Everything that usually lives in WhatsApp, notebooks, and someone’s memory.

Start with what your family already does today: collect money, pay school fees, run projects, track farm activity, hold meetings, and keep important records safe.

1

Shared finance ledger

Track contributions, expenses, payment accounts, and fee activity in a way the family can review together without guesswork.

2

Projects and farming

Follow family projects, operational tasks, farm records, outputs, and actual spending without splitting work across separate tools.

3

Meetings and votes

Schedule meetings, write minutes, open proposals, and keep decisions visible long after the family gathering ends.

4

Emergency and school fees

Handle urgent needs and recurring obligations with dedicated workflows instead of mixing everything into one vague balance.

5

Assets and vault

Keep track of land, equipment, income-generating assets, and important family files in one trusted family register.

6

Reports and AI support

Generate summaries for family meetings and get contextual guidance from FamilyOS without replacing human judgment.
